mayhem.tax
Tokens Launch FAQ Profile
What is mayhem.tax?

mayhem.tax is a layer built on top of the mayhem protocol that allows you to create tax tokens using the 2022 standard on pump.fun. The mayhem protocol enables random transactions, custom buy/sell taxes, and advanced token features.

What is the mayhem protocol?

mayhem is an upcoming protocol from pump.fun that will transition to the 2022 standard, allowing for random transactions, custom buy/sell tax rates, and other advanced token mechanics.

When will mayhem.tax launch?

mayhem.tax will launch once pump.fun officially deploys the mayhem contract. Until then, the platform is in preparation mode.

What are tax tokens?

Tax tokens are tokens that charge a percentage fee on each buy and/or sell transaction. These fees can be used for various purposes like liquidity, rewards, or project development.

What is the 2022 standard?

The 2022 standard refers to a token standard on Solana that supports advanced features like transfer hooks, which enable tax mechanisms and other programmable behaviors.

Can I set custom tax rates?

Yes, you can set separate buy and sell tax rates (0-25% each) when launching your token. You can also enable random tax variations for additional functionality.

What are random transactions?

Random transactions are a feature of the mayhem protocol that allows for randomized tax rates or other behaviors, adding unpredictability and unique mechanics to your token.
